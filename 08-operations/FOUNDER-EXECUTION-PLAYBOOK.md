## APPENDIX: COUNCIL OF EXPERTS - STRUCTURED DECISION FRAMEWORK

### Overview: Multi-Dimensional Decision Review

The **Council of Experts** is a structured review process where 5 domain experts evaluate strategic decisions across key business phases. This ensures multi-dimensional excellence and reduces blind spots.

**Council Members:**
1. **Strategic Advisor** - Business/GTM/positioning expertise
2. **Product Architect** - Product/UX/user research expertise
3. **Engineering Lead** - Technical/scale/infrastructure expertise
4. **Growth Hacker** - Execution/metrics/revenue expertise
5. **Researcher/Intelligence Officer** - Market research/competitive intelligence

### Phase-Based Review Framework

For each major strategic decision:

**Strategic Advisor Reviews:**
- Is positioning clear and defensible?
- Is market timing realistic?
- Does this decision align with TAM and competitive strategy?
- What's the risk/reward?

**Product Architect Reviews:**
- Is this product-grounded and customer-validated?
- Does this advance product-market fit?
- What's the customer impact?

**Engineering Lead Reviews:**
- Is this technically feasible?
- What's the engineering effort and timeline?
- What infrastructure/scaling implications?

**Growth Hacker Reviews:**
- What's the revenue/growth impact?
- How do we measure success?
- What's the metrics dashboard?

**Researcher Reviews:**
- What's the competitive threat level?
- Is market data supporting this decision?
- What are we missing from market research?
