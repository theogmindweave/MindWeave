### Healthcare Sales Cycle Progression

- Research target's AI initiatives (LinkedIn, job postings, press releases)

- 30-min call with CPO/CISO
- Listen: What are their AI initiatives? What keeps them awake?
- Key question: "What's your current approach to AI governance?"
- Goal: Get commitment to 2-hour discovery meeting

**Week 5-8: Discovery Phase (2 meetings)**
- Meeting 1 (90 min): Deep dive on compliance requirements, audit needs, pain points
- Meeting 2 (60 min): Technical requirements, integration with existing tools
- Deliverable: Compliance playbook specific to their situation (custom document)
- Goal: Get agreement to run pilot

**Week 9-12: Pilot (30 days)**
- Scope: Monitor 1-2 AI models, generate compliance report
- Deliverable: Week 2 interim audit trail, Week 4 full compliance report
- Success metric: "Can we audit [model name]'s governance?" = YES
- Goal: Pilot success leads to full deployment discussion

**Week 13-16: Due Diligence**
- Security questionnaire response (100+ questions)
- SOC 2 Type II audit (if required)
- Legal review of custom BAA terms
- Compliance team evaluation meeting
- Goal: Get CPO approval + CFO budget sign-off

**Week 17-20: Negotiation & Close**
- Negotiate contract terms (multi-year discount, SLA, support)
- Sign BAA, MSA, DPA (Data Processing Agreement)
- Close deal
- Typical discount: 15-20% for multi-year commitment

- Cost to achieve: $150K-$300K (expensive, but covers all agencies)
- Win rate: 70%+ (once FedRAMP certified, all agencies can use)
- **Strategy:** Invest in FedRAMP once, sell to 100+ agencies

## EXPANSION MECHANICS: THE MASTER PLAYBOOK

### Universal Expansion Triggers (Apply to All Verticals)

**Trigger #1: Usage Threshold Exceeded**
- Customer hits token limit / API call limit
- Automatic notification: "You're at 95% of your plan limit"
- Conversation: "Let's review your plan and see if you need to upgrade"
- Typical upgrade: +$50K-$200K depending on vertical

**Trigger #2: New Department / Team Adoption**
- Marketing department wants to use platform
- Separate from initial finance/compliance deployment
- Conversation: "Great news! Your marketing team can benefit too. Here's pricing for an additional team seat"
- Typical expansion: +$25K-$100K

**Trigger #3: Regulatory Requirement Update**
- New law passed (state privacy, industry regulation)
- Conversation: "New requirement for [regulation]. We added [feature]. You'll need to upgrade."
- Typical expansion: +$50K-$150K

**Trigger #4: Customer Company Milestone**
- Series A funding (growth signal)
- International expansion (complexity)
- Merger or acquisition (sudden scale)
- Conversation triggered by Crunchbase alert / LinkedIn announcement
- Typical expansion: +$100K-$300K

**Trigger #5: Competitive Threat**
- Competitor deployed at customer site
- Conversation: "We heard you're evaluating [competitor]. Let's talk about why we're better for you."
- Typical expansion: +$50K-$200K (convert trial to paid, or expand)
