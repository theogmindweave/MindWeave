# Council of Experts - Review Protocol & Checklist

## Overview

The Council of Experts is a structured review process where 5 domain experts evaluate MindWeave's strategic decisions across 10 upgrade phases. This ensures multi-dimensional excellence and reduces blind spots.

**Council Members:**
1. **Strategic Advisor** - Business/GTM/positioning expertise
2. **Product Architect** - Product/UX/user research expertise
3. **Engineering Lead** - Technical/scale/infrastructure expertise
4. **Growth Hacker** - Execution/metrics/revenue expertise
5. **Researcher/Intelligence Officer** - Market research/competitive intelligence

---

## Phase-by-Phase Review Checklists

### PHASE 1: Research Synthesis & Gap Analysis
**Owner:** Researcher Lead
**Duration:** 2-3 days
**Council Review Attendees:** Researcher (lead), Strategic Advisor

#### Strategic Advisor Checklist
- [ ] **Completeness:** Are all 11 competitors analyzed with threat assessment?
- [ ] **Positioning clarity:** Is our differentiation vs. WorkWeave/MintMCP clear?
- [ ] **Market timing:** Is 18-24 month window realistic before AWS/Azure/GCP move?
- [ ] **TAM validation:** Is $21B TAM justified with multiple sizing methodologies?
- [ ] **Regulatory drivers:** Are compliance mandates clearly connected to demand?
- [ ] **Risk assessment:** Have we identified top 5 market risks?
- [ ] **Trend alignment:** Do we understand the 10 key industry trends?

**Sign-off:** Strategic Advisor must confirm positioning defensibility

#### Researcher Checklist
- [ ] **Source validation:** Are all sources recent and authoritative?
- [ ] **Competitive data:** Have we validated 11 competitor profiles?
- [ ] **Customer interviews:** Do 8 interviews represent diverse personas?
- [ ] **Gap identification:** Are high-priority research gaps called out?
- [ ] **LinkedIn analysis:** Is WorkWeave's 179-post strategy understood?
- [ ] **Regulatory accuracy:** Are regional compliance requirements current (2025 laws)?
- [ ] **Open-source learning:** Are 8 reference repos deeply analyzed?

**Sign-off:** Researcher must confirm data quality and completeness

**Council Decision:** Approve Phase 1 → Continue to Phase 2, OR Request revisions

**Owner:** Strategic Lead
**Duration:** 2-3 days
**Council Review Attendees:** Strategic Advisor (lead), Researcher, Product Architect

#### Strategic Advisor Checklist

- [ ] **Competition viability:** Can we win against WorkWeave despite 10k+ existing users?

**Sign-off:** Strategic Advisor must confirm positioning is differentiated and defensible

- [ ] **Customer pain mapping:** Does our positioning address top 5 customer pain points?
- [ ] **MVP alignment:** Is Phase 2 positioning achievable in MVP?

**Sign-off:** Product Architect must confirm positioning is product-grounded

- [ ] **Regulatory tailwind:** Is AI governance regulation creating demand advantage?

**Sign-off:** Researcher must confirm competitive threat assessment is realistic

## Council Review Meeting Format

### Preparation (24 hours before)
- Owner completes phase deliverables
- Owner creates 1-page summary highlighting decisions and trade-offs
- Each council member reviews summary independently
- Council members write down top 3 questions/concerns

### Meeting (1 hour)
1. **Owner presentation** (10 min): High-level summary, key decisions, alternatives considered
2. **Council Q&A** (20 min): Each council member asks top questions
3. **Discussion** (20 min): Trade-off discussion, risk assessment
4. **Decision** (10 min): Approve, Revise, or Escalate

### Documentation
- Meeting notes recorded (decisions, action items, owner of any follow-ups)
- Council sign-off comment in git commit message
- Phase marked as Approved/Revision-Needed in tracking spreadsheet

---

## Escalation Path

### If Council Cannot Reach Consensus
1. Escalate to CEO for final decision
2. Document dissenting opinions in git commit
3. CEO's decision recorded and committed
4. Proceed with decision or pause phase

### If Phase Fails Council Review
1. Owner identifies revisions needed
2. Create sub-task for revisions
3. Re-review in 1 week
4. Escalate if still cannot approve
