## SECTION 2: CASE STUDY TYPES & DEPLOYMENT STRATEGY

### Type 1: The Problem-Solution-Result (PSR) Case Study

**Format:** 1-2 page document + 1 video

**Structure:**
- **Problem (1/3):** Customer's initial situation (pain, scale, risk)
- **Solution (1/3):** Exactly what you did (features, timeline, implementation)
- **Result (1/3):** Quantified outcome (metrics, ROI, strategic benefit)

```

- No audit trail for regulatory review

SOLUTION:
MindWeave governance platform:
- Deployed in 3 weeks (vs 9 months for custom build)
- Automated audit trail for all 50 AI models
- Real-time compliance monitoring
- Dashboard for regulatory review

RESULT:
- 100% audit-ready in 3 months
- 160 hours per quarter saved (compliance team)
- Zero audit findings related to AI
- $600K+ saved vs custom build
- Board confidence in AI safety strategy

Quote: "MindWeave gave us governance confidence. Our board now trusts our AI deployment." - Chief Compliance Officer
```

**Deployment:**
- Homepage: Featured case study (rotate monthly)

## SECTION 3: CASE STUDY DEVELOPMENT PROCESS

### Step 1: Customer Selection (Identify Best Candidates)

**Ideal Case Study Customer Has:**
- ✅ **Strong Results:** 3+ quantified outcomes (ROI, hours saved, risk reduced)
- ✅ **Good Retention:** 12+ months with us (staying proves value)
- ✅ **Willingness:** Agrees to be named and share story
- ✅ **Prestige:** Recognizable brand (Mayo > "Regional Hospital")
- ✅ **Replicable:** Story applies to other prospects
- ✅ **Speed:** Went from decision to value quickly (shows what's possible)

**Red flags to avoid:**
- ❌ Customer just signed (haven't proven value yet)
- ❌ Customer unhappy (might leave, bad reference)
- ❌ Generic story (doesn't help other prospects)
- ❌ Niche situation (other prospects can't relate)
- ❌ Demands anonymity (can't use as case study)

**Selection Process (Monthly):**
1. **Review active customers** (your best performers)
2. **Identify 3-5 candidates** with strong metrics
3. **Call customer:** "We'd love to document your success story. Interested?"
4. **Get commitment:** Written agreement to participate
5. **Plan timeline:** 4-6 weeks from kickoff to published

**Target:** 2-3 new case studies per quarter = 8-12 per year

---

### Step 2: Information Gathering (Research Interview)

**The 60-Minute Interview:**

**Minutes 0-5: Rapport & Framing**
- "Thanks for agreeing to share your story."
- "This will help other enterprises understand how to do this."
- "We'll feature this on our website + use in sales conversations."
- "You'll have final approval before we publish."

**Minutes 5-20: Problem Discovery (Listen 80%)**
- "Walk me through your situation when you started with us."
- "What was the pain point?"
- "How was it affecting your business?"
- "What was the cost of not solving it?"
- Listen for: Specific numbers, emotional impact, business consequence

**Minutes 20-35: Solution Deep-Dive**
- "Talk me through how you deployed MindWeave."
- "What features mattered most?"
- "How did your team respond?"
- "Any surprises during implementation?"
- Listen for: Easy to implement, team liked it, fast value realization

**Minutes 35-50: Results & ROI**
- "What metrics improved?"
- "What's the financial impact?" (hours saved, risk reduced, revenue enabled)
- "What changed strategically?"
- "How would you compare to the alternative (custom build, competitor)?"
- Listen for: Quantified outcomes, competitive positioning, strategic benefit

**Minutes 50-60: Quotes & Messaging**
- "How would you describe MindWeave to a peer?"
- "What's the key thing other [industry] companies should know?"
- "Would you do this again? Why?"
- "Anything you'd change about the process?"
- Listen for: Natural quotes, key messaging, authenticity

**Recording & Notes:**
- Record interview (with permission)
- Take detailed notes
- Transcript (AI transcription tool: otter.ai, rev.com)
- Pull key quotes verbatim

---

### Step 3: Case Study Writing

**Writing Process:**

**Outline (30 min)**
1. Problem: 2-3 paragraphs
2. Solution: 2-3 paragraphs
3. Results: 2-3 paragraphs
4. Quotes: 2-3 key quotes
5. Lessons: 1 paragraph
6. CTA: Final call-to-action

**First Draft (2-3 hours)**
- Write naturally from interview notes
- Tell a story (not just facts)
- Include quotes throughout
- Emphasize quantified results
- Lead with biggest surprise/win

**Client Review (3-5 days)**
- Send draft to customer contact
- Request: Fact check, any edits, quote approval
- Usually get feedback within 48 hours
- Make revisions based on feedback

**Final Version (1-2 hours)**
- Incorporate client feedback
- Polish writing
- Add formatting (headers, bolded results)
- Create 1-2 page version + longer version
- Proof-read multiple times

**Design & Graphics (2-3 hours)**
- Create professional PDF layout
- Add company logo
- Include customer photo (if possible)
- Add pull-out quotes (design element)
- Create downloadable version

**Total time:** 1-2 weeks from interview to published
