## SECTION 1: WHY PARTNERSHIPS ARE YOUR HIGHEST-ROI CHANNEL

### Partnership Math vs Direct Sales

**Direct Sales (What We Discussed in ITER 17):**
```
Effort: 600 hours per enterprise deal
CAC: $15K-$30K per enterprise customer
Conversion rate: 30-40% pilot to customer
Timeline: 150-180 days
Revenue per deal: $250K ACV

Year 1 target: 10-15 enterprise customers = $2.5M-$5M ARR
Effort: 6,000-9,000 hours (3-4.5 people year-round)
```

**Partnership Sales (This Playbook):**
```
Effort per deal: 100-200 hours (partner does heavy lifting)
CAC: $5K-$10K (partner shares acquisition cost)
Conversion rate: 40-60% (partner vouches for you)
Timeline: 60-90 days (partner accelerates)
Revenue per deal: $250K ACV (same)

Year 1 target: 10-15 partnership customers = $2.5M-$5M ARR
Effort: 1,000-3,000 hours (0.5-1.5 people)
Resource efficiency: 5-10x better than direct
```

**Key Insight:** Partnerships reduce your CAC and effort by 5-10x while keeping deal size the same. This is the highest-ROI growth channel for enterprise SaaS.
