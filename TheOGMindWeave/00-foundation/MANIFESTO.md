## What We Believe

### 1. AI Should Evolve WITH Teams, Not Be Imposed ON Them

> "The best AI tools learn from how you work, not force you to work their way."

Every team is different. Every developer has preferences. AI tools should adapt to the humans, not the other way around.

### 2. Governance Should Be Invisible

> "The best security is the kind you don't notice."

Compliance, audit trails, access control - these should work silently in the background. Developers should feel empowered, not policed.

### 3. Measurement Enables Improvement

> "You can't improve what you can't measure."

Teams need visibility into their AI usage. Not to micromanage, but to understand. What's working? What's not? Where's the ROI?

### 4. Intelligence Compounds

> "Every interaction makes the system smarter."

The APIR principle: Adapt, Prune, Improve, Repeat. MindWeave learns from every accept, every reject, every pattern. Over time, it becomes uniquely tuned to your organization.

### 5. Open Beats Closed

> "Transparency builds trust."

We believe in open protocols (MCP), open standards (DORA), and open communication. Where possible, we open-source. We contribute back.

---

## The APIR Philosophy

**A**dapt - Learn from behavior, feedback, patterns
**P**rune - Remove what doesn't work, focus on what does
**I**mprove - Iterate, refine, get better every cycle
**R**epeat - Never stop learning, never stop evolving

APIR is not just a framework. It's how we build. It's how our product works. It's how we think.

## Our Principles

### For the Product

1. **Simple over complex** - If it takes more than 10 minutes to understand, it's too complicated.

2. **Value over features** - Ship what matters. Kill what doesn't.

3. **Enterprise-ready from day one** - Security, compliance, scale are not afterthoughts.

4. **Developer experience matters** - If developers hate it, it fails.

### For the Company

1. **Speed over perfection** - Ship fast, learn faster.

2. **Focus over breadth** - Do one thing exceptionally well.

3. **Customer over ego** - Build what they need, not what we want.

4. **Sustainability over hype** - Build a real business, not a story.

### For the Team

1. **Trust over control** - Hire great people, let them work.

2. **Transparency over secrecy** - Share information freely.

3. **Results over hours** - Outcomes matter, not time spent.

4. **Learning over knowing** - Stay curious, stay humble.
