# MindWeave Milestones

> The key checkpoints on our journey from 0 to $100M ARR

---

## Overview

This document outlines MindWeave's critical milestones across product, business, and organizational dimensions. Each milestone is designed to be measurable, time-bound, and aligned with our fundraising and growth strategy.

Milestones serve two purposes: internal accountability and external communication (investors, customers, partners).

## Year 1 Milestones (2025)

### M1: MVP Launch

**Target Date:** Q1 2025 (March)

**Description:**
Ship the minimum viable product to the first design partners.

**Success Criteria:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Core Features Shipped | 5 (Dashboard, Teams, MCP Registry, Audit, SSO) |
| Design Partners | 10 signed agreements |
| Uptime | 99.5% |
| Critical Bugs | 0 at launch |

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Token usage dashboard (real-time)
- [ ] Team/user management
- [ ] MCP registry (read-only)
- [ ] Basic audit logging
- [ ] Okta/Azure AD SSO

**Dependencies:**
- Founding engineers hired (2 minimum)
- Infrastructure setup (AWS/GCP)
- Design partner agreements signed

**Risk Factors:**
- Engineering capacity constraints
- Scope creep delaying launch
- Design partner availability

---

### M2: Seed Round Close

**Target Date:** Q2 2025 (May)

**Description:**
Close seed financing to fund 18-month runway.

**Success Criteria:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Amount Raised | $8-12M |
| Pre-Money Valuation | $40-50M |
| Lead Investor | Enterprise-focused VC |
| Time to Close | <3 months |

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Term sheet signed
- [ ] Due diligence completed
- [ ] Funds wired
- [ ] Board formed

**Investor Milestones Required:**
- MVP launched (M1)
- 10 design partners
- 3+ customer references
- Competitive analysis complete

**Risk Factors:**
- Market conditions
- Competitive term sheets
- Timing with MVP

### M3: First 10 Pilot Customers

**Target Date:** Q2 2025 (June)

**Description:**
Convert 10 design partners to pilot customers with real usage.

**Success Criteria:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Active Pilots | 10 customers |
| Weekly Active Users | 50+ across all pilots |
| NPS | 40+ (early product) |
| Features Requested | Documented |

**Pilot Customer Criteria:**
- Active Claude usage (100k+ tokens/month)
- At least 10 users on platform
- Weekly check-in calls scheduled
- Willing to provide testimonial

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Pilot agreements signed
- [ ] Customers onboarded
- [ ] Success metrics dashboard
- [ ] Feedback loop established

**Risk Factors:**
- Pilot-to-paid conversion unclear
- Feature requests overwhelming
- Customer success capacity

- Pricing pushback
