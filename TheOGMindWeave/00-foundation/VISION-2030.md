## The MindWeave.World Vision

### Why a Marketplace?

**Problem:**
- Teams rebuild the same MCPs repeatedly
- No way to share best practices across industry
- Slow adoption of new AI capabilities
- Vendor lock-in with no alternatives

**Solution: MindWeave.World**
- **Discovery:** Find MCPs, skills, plugins you need
- **Sharing:** Share what you build (public or private)
- **Monetization:** Sell premium MCPs and skills
- **Learning:** Courses, docs, videos, certifications
- **Community:** Forums, support, networking
