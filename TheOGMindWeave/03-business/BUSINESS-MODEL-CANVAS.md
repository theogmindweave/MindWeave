## Executive Summary

MindWeave operates a B2B SaaS business model, providing enterprise AI governance infrastructure for organizations deploying Claude at scale. Our platform-based approach creates value through operational efficiency, compliance automation, and cost optimization, capturing value via tiered subscription pricing based on usage and capabilities.
