## 7. Deal Structure Considerations

### 7.1 Acquisition Deal Structures

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    DEAL STRUCTURE OPTIONS                                │
│                                                                         │
│   ALL-CASH DEAL                                                         │
│   ├── Advantages: Certainty, liquidity, simple                          │
│   ├── Disadvantages: May be lower headline number                       │
│   └── Typical: 70-80% of mid-market deals                               │
│                                                                         │
│   CASH + STOCK                                                          │
│   ├── Advantages: Higher headline, tax deferral on stock                │
│   ├── Disadvantages: Stock risk, lockup periods                         │
│   └── Typical structure: 60-70% cash, 30-40% stock                      │
│                                                                         │
│   EARNOUT STRUCTURE                                                     │
│   ├── Advantages: Bridges valuation gap                                 │
│   ├── Disadvantages: Execution risk, metric disputes                    │
│   └── Typical: 10-25% of deal in earnout                                │
│                                                                         │
│   RETENTION PACKAGES                                                    │
│   ├── Typically 10-20% of deal value                                    │
│   ├── 2-4 year vesting for key employees                                │
│   └── Important for maintaining team through transition                 │
│                                                                         │
│   ESCROW/HOLDBACK                                                       │
│   ├── Typically 5-15% of purchase price                                 │
│   ├── 12-24 month release period                                        │
│   └── Covers rep & warranty claims                                      │
│                                                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 7.2 Key Negotiation Points

| Term | Seller Preference | Buyer Preference |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| Cash/Stock Mix | More cash | More stock |
| Earnout | Shorter, achievable | Longer, stretch |
| Escrow | Lower %, shorter | Higher %, longer |
| Retention | Smaller pool | Larger pool |
| Rep & Warranty | Limited, short | Broad, long |
| Non-compete | Narrower | Broader |
