## 1Ô∏è‚É£ PRICING STRATEGY

### Pricing Tiers

**Freemium (Free)**
- Up to 10 team members
- Basic dashboard (30-day rolling)
- No compliance features
- Community support
- **Goal:** Viral adoption, inbound generation

**Professional ($300/month per team)**
- Up to 50 team members
- Full dashboard + cost attribution
- Audit logs (90-day retention)
- Email support
- **Target:** Series B/C SaaS (100-500 engineers)

**Enterprise ($800-1,500/month per team)**
- Unlimited team members
- Full features + compliance roadmap
- Extended audit logs (6+ years)
- Dedicated support + weekly check-ins
- Custom integrations
- On-prem option (future)
- **Target:** Large enterprises + regulated industries

### Pricing Justification

**By Segment:**
- **SMB (50-200 emp):** $100/month/seat (or flat $2K/month)
- **Mid-Market (200-2K emp):** $300/month/seat (or flat $30K/month)
- **Enterprise (2K+ emp):** $800-1,500/month/seat (or custom based on seat count)

**Compliance Premium:**
- HIPAA customers: 2x base price (government mandate)
- SOX customers: 1.5x base price (financial requirement)
- GDPR customers: 1.2x base price (regulatory pressure)

## 4Ô∏è‚É£ UNIT ECONOMICS

### LTV:CAC Analysis

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
- Design partner incentive: $5K discount (one-time)
- Sales commission (5%): $2K per $40K ACV deal
- Marketing spend: $3K per customer acquired
- **Total CAC:** $10K per customer

**Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- ARPU: $300/month
- Lifetime: 36 months (3-year contract average)
- Expansion revenue: 20% yearly growth
- **Base LTV:** $10,800 (without expansion)
- **Expanded LTV:** $16,200 (with expansion)

**LTV:CAC Ratio:**
- **Year 1:** 1.6:1 (acceptable, growth phase)
- **Year 2:** 4:1 (healthy)
- **Year 3:** 8:1 (excellent, expansion drives margin)

**Target:** Achieve 5:1 ratio by Month 12, 8:1 by Month 24
