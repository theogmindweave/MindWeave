## RED FLAGS TO STOP & FIX

If ANY of these happen, stop everything and fix immediately:

1. **Churn Spikes >2%**
   - Diagnosis: Why are customers leaving?
   - Action: Customer success emergency

2. **Win Rate Drops <15%**
   - Diagnosis: Demo broken? Pricing too high? Competitive threat?
   - Action: Demo review + competitive analysis

3. **MRR Declining >10% MoM**
   - Diagnosis: Revenue or customer issue?
   - Action: Sales audit + churn analysis

4. **NPS Drops >5 points**
   - Diagnosis: Product issue? Service issue?
   - Action: Customer interviews + product audit

5. **Key Team Member Departure**
   - Diagnosis: Why are they leaving?
   - Action: Succession plan + retention review

6. **Runway <12 months**
   - Diagnosis: Burn too high or revenue too low?
   - Action: Fundraising acceleration or cost cutting
