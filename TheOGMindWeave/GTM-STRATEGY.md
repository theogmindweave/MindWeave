## Market Positioning

**ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):**
- Enterprise tech companies (500+ engineers)
- Rapid Claude integration (2+ internal use cases)
- High MCP duplication problem (50+ MCPs)
- Budget: $50K-$250K+ annually

**Competitive Positioning:**
- vs. WorkWeave: Governance-first (not observability-first)
- vs. MintMCP: Team-based controls + hivemind discovery
- vs. AWS/Microsoft: Specialized for Claude + MCPs (2-4 week deployment)

---

## Sales Process (8-20 Week Cycle)

**Stage 1: Prospecting (Week 1-2)**
- Identify high-MCP-count companies
- Research Claude adoption (GitHub, Slack, community)
- Inbound from partnership or content

**Stage 2: Discovery (Week 3-4)**
- Customer call: What's your MCP problem?
- Quantify: How many MCPs? What's the waste?
- Identify: Who's decision maker? Budget holder?

**Stage 3: Solution (Week 5-8)**
- Demo Hivemind Discovery
- Show $18-47K annual savings potential
- Create business case (ROI, payback period)

**Stage 4: Negotiation (Week 9-16)**
- Pricing discussion ($50K-$250K+, depends on size)
- Contract review
- Security/compliance questions

**Stage 5: Close (Week 17-20)**
- Legal sign-off
- Purchase order
- Customer onboarded (Week 1 success)

## Key Metrics

- Pipeline: $X MRR in qualified deals
- Win rate: % of deals that close
- ACV: Average contract value (target: $50K+)
- CAC: Cost per customer acquired
- LTV: Lifetime value (target: $250K+ for 36 months)
