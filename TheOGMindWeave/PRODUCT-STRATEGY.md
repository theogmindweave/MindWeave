## Product Vision

**Problem:** Organizations have 50+ duplicate MCPs, no governance, massive waste ($200K+/year)
**Solution:** Single platform that discovers, consolidates, and manages MCPs
**Impact:** $18-47K annual savings per customer

---

## MVP Scope (Weeks 1-4)

**Core Feature: Hivemind Discovery**
- Scans customer's MCP inventory
- Identifies duplicates and consolidation opportunities
- Recommends consolidation actions
- Calculates savings potential

**Secondary Features:**
- MCP governance (access control, approval workflows)
- Usage tracking (which MCPs actually used)
- Cost monitoring (dollars spent on duplicates)
